Nagaraju Jalasutram

Government Teacher | Dedicated Educator

About

Nagaraju Jalasutram was born on 24 July 1979 in Vadlamanu village, Agiripalli Mandal, Eluru District. Coming from a humble and backward village, he worked hard to build a successful career in education.

He is currently working as a Government Teacher in Eluru District and has dedicated many years to shaping students' lives.
